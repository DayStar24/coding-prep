## Math Fundamentals Lesson 1  
```nohighlight
Question 1:

1 + 2 * 3 - 4

Answer: 3

1 + 2 * 3 - 4

1 + 6 - 4

7 - 4 = 3  

Question 2:

(1 + 2) * 3 - 4

Answer: 5

(1 + 2) * 3 - 4

3 * 3 - 4

9 - 4 = 5 

Question 3:

8 - 4  / 2 + 1

Answer: 7

8 - 4 / 2 + 1

8 - 2 + 1

6 + 1 = 7

Question 4:

8 - 6 / (2 + 1)

Answer: 6

8 - 6 / (2 + 1)

8 - 6 / 3

8 - 2 = 6

Question 5:

8 + 7 * 4 / 2

Answer: 22

8 + 7 * 4 / 2

8 + 28 / 2

8 + 14 = 22

Question 6:

(8 + 7) * 4 / 2

Answer: 30

(8 + 7) * 4 / 2

15 * 4 / 2

60 / 2 = 30

Question 7:

((20 + 10) - (6 * 2))

Answer: 18

((20 + 10) - (6 * 2))

(30 - 12) = 18

Question 8:

9 - 2 * (14 - 10) + 6

Answer: 7

9 - 2 * (14 - 10) + 6

9 - 2 * 4 + 6

9 - 8 + 6

1 + 6 = 7

((2 + 1) + (7 - 3) * (4 / 2))

((2 + 1) + (7 - 3) * (4 / 2))
```  

## Math Fundamentals Lesson 2  
```nohighlight
Question 1:

2 ^ 4

Answer: 16

2 ^ 4

2 * 2 * 2 * 2

4 * 4 = 16

  
Question 2:

4 ^ 1

Answer: 4

  
Question 3:

6 ^ 0

Answer: 1  Any number raised to the 0 power equals 1.

  
Question 4:

-2 ^ 3

Answer: -8

(-2) * (-2) * (-2)

4 * (-2) = -8
 
  
Question 5:

-3 ^ 3

Answer: -27

(-3) * (-3) * (-3)

9 * (-3) = -27
 
  
Question 6:

3 ^ 3 ^ 3

Answer: 19,683

3 ^ 3 ^ 3

(3 * 3 * 3) ^ 3

27 ^ 3

27 * 27 * 27 = 19,683
 
  
Question 7:

0 ^ 0

Answer: 1  Any number raised to the 0 power equals 1, even if that number is 0.
```
